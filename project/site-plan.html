Site Plan - My Brother's Bakery

Site Name

Coni Bakery - Chosen because it is friendly, easy to remember, and reflects the identity of our family-owned bakery.

Site Purpose

This website provides information about the bakery, including product offerings, categories of baked goods, and basic business information. It also allows customers to place orders or contact the bakery directly. The project is meaningful because it is based on a real family business.

Scenarios

  • What types of cakes and baked goods are available, and what are their prices?
  • How can I place an order for a birthday or special event?
  • Where can I find the bakery's contact information or location?

Color Scheme

  • #c7b69f - site background
  • #553d2a - headings and main text
  • #ab9680 - accents and highlights

Typography

  • Montserrat - used throughout the document for headings and paragraphs
  • Sleep Night - used in the wireframe to highlight sections
